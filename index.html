About Me

I currently serve as a Senior Image Algorithm Engineer at Soul App, where I research on real-time video generation and compact video tokenizer. I possess extensive cross-disciplinary research experience spanning computer vision, multimodal systems, and large language models. Prior to joining Soul, I worked at a technology startup focusing on the development and optimization of visual algorithms. I received my master's degree in Software Engineering from the University of Sydney, where my research concentrated on fine-grained visual categorization techniques, which established a solid academic foundation for my professional development.
